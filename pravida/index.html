Key Features

Privacy is the default. Encryption is the foundation.

🔐

End-to-End Encryption

All messages, calls, and files encrypted using AES-256-GCM with HKDF session keys via CryptoKit. Nobody reads your messages — not even us.

🎟️

Invite-Only Networks

Create or join private networks via cryptographic invite links and QR codes. Your network, your rules, your people.

📡

Mesh Networking

Device-to-device communication via Bluetooth and WiFi when you're offline. No internet required — messages still get through.

📞

Encrypted Voice Calls

1:1 encrypted voice calls with native CallKit integration. Crystal clear audio, fully private.

🧠

On-Device AI

Smart replies, message summaries, and content moderation powered by Apple Intelligence. All processing stays on your device.

👥

Trust Model

Tiered membership — Owner, Admin, Member, Guest — with granular permissions. You decide who sees what.

💨

Disappearing Messages

Signal-style configurable auto-delete timers with cross-device sync. Messages vanish when you want them to.

📸

Screenshot Protection

Detection and notification to the sender when content is screenshotted. Know when your privacy is at stake.

🛡️

Identity Verification

Safety numbers display and QR code scanning for in-person verification. Confirm who you're really talking to.

🔍

Message Search

Keyword search with result highlighting across all conversations. Find anything, instantly.

📤

Share Extension

Share images, text, URLs, and video from any app directly into Pravida. Seamless integration with iOS.

💾

Encrypted Backup

AES-GCM encrypted backup export and import. Your data is protected even in transit and at rest.
